Our Love Story

Where It All Started: Zambales

28 April 2018

Having been acquainted during our high school days, after a few months of further self-discovery, we organized our first travel to Zambales. During this occasion, Mike eagerly anticipated marking his birthday during the journey, and thus, our plan came into fruition, resulting in unforgettable memories. Mike fondly recalls it as his "best birthday so far." Little did we realize that this very experience would catalyze a more profound bond between us, ultimately guiding us to the juncture we find ourselves in today.

First Lie - Seminar

01 July 2018

"Ma, may seminar kami sa Manila sa Friday until Sunday" was the initial untruth uttered, creating space for Mike's heart, which appeared to be perpetually drawn in a northerly direction. Little did they know, it's Mike's excuse for visiting Sarah in Manila. In an instant, Mike's intrigue for seminars heightened, irrespective of the distance or expenses involved – whatever it takes.

Relationship Status Update: It's a confirmed "Yes."

12 June 2018

On June 12, it became our personal independence day, as we officially labeled what we've been nurturing for the past few months. It's a mutual agreement for both of us since we've both chosen to commit to this relationship. We've firmly decided that the two of us are fully embracing this partnership. It's a sealed deal, no doubt about it.

All in! Through Ups and Downs

-

As a couple, we encountered a variety of challenges, both individually and together. One of the significant adjustments was when Mike relocated to Manila to further his career and strengthen our relationship. Meanwhile, Sarah was introduced to Mike's closest friends and opted to live with them. Sarah seamlessly integrated with them and formed new friendships.
However, it wasn't always smooth sailing for us. As we delved deeper into our relationship and got to know each other better, we discovered that some aspects didn't align with our individual principles and values. Despite these differences, we made a steadfast commitment to prioritize "Us."
When the pandemic struck, we were all together and weathered the storm as a unit, supporting one another until we eventually made the decision to return to our respective homes.

Journey as Furparents

12 September 2020

We're thrilled to announce that we've become parents! Our adorable fur babies, Anga, Butter, and Sokka, have entered our lives, and with them came the responsibility of parenthood. We've been diligently feeding them, looking after their well-being, and tending to them, especially when they're under the weather (those vet bills and those 3 a.m. medication wake-ups, ugh). However, despite the challenges, we're immensely grateful and overjoyed to have them as cherished members of our family.

Endgame: Zambales (The Proposal)

12 June 2022

It's the endgame for us! We're concluding our journey as boyfriend and girlfriend, but this is where it all began. We are absolutely certain about the love we share and the future we envision. Mike's proposal has marked the beginning of a fresh chapter in our lives as we embark on a new adventure together. We invite you to join us on this significant milestone and celebrate this joyous occasion with us.
